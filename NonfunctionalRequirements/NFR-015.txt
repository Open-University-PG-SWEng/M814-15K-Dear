REQ-ID: NFR-015

Description: The product shall make the allowable return of unwanted tickets by customers as easy as possible

Type: 11 Usability and Humanity

Rationale: To enable customers to return tickets open for reallocation as quickly and seamlessly as possible, to avoid unwanted tickets going unused and thus avoid leaving gaps in the crowd

Fit Criterion: The check by the application back end to ensure return for those tickets is still open shall be carried out in no more than three seconds for 95% of return transactions
